Odyssey - Extended Operations

Some cameras have reversed output channels. In this case, it is necessary to restore the correct ordering by software. Other cameras have blind areas (e.g. vertical black columns 1 pixel wide). In this case, usually the blind columns are masked by duplicating the nearest neighbour columns onto the blind ones.

These operations that are needed in special cases are called Extended Operations in the Odyssey terminology. For each of these operations, the source and destination areas and the type of operation need to be specified. The results of such operations are shown below.
